## Overview

This repository contains a collection of 3D-printable STL files for functional and mechanical use.

The project includes a mix of:
- Original models created through independent CAD design
- Modified or adapted components based on GPL or other compatible licensed source projects

All combined works are distributed under the terms of the GNU General Public License v3.0 (GPL-3.0), where applicable.

---

## Design Approach

Models in this repository may originate from:
- Independently developed models based on functional constraints or prior art
- Iterative modifications of GPL or other compatible-licensed open-source components
- Hybrid assemblies combining original and GPL or other compatible-licensed derived elements

Where GPL or other compatible licensed components are used, their original licenses and attribution are preserved in the project documentation.

Functional compatibility requirements may influence geometry, particularly in interface regions such as mounting points or alignment features. Where parts must meet specific physical constraints (such as mounting alignment or interface fit), any similarity to other objects or systems may result from functional constraints only.
